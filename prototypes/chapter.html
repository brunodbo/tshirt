What's a photochrom?

Published primarily from the 1890s to 1910s, these prints were created by the Photoglob Company in Zurich, Switzerland, and the Detroit Publishing Company in Michigan. The richly colored images look like photographs but are actually ink-based photolithographs. To view more, check out the Photochrom Print Collection at the Library of Congress
