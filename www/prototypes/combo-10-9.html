Bangladesh makes a lot of the clothes people in America wear.

You've bought pants from JC Penney, underwear from Target, lots of other retailers. There's a good chance they were made in Bangladesh.

There are 4 million people working in the garment industry today in Bangladesh, quadruple what it was just 10 years ago. Twenty years ago­ there was essentially no one.

To understand why 4 million people are making this choice. making the choice to come to an overcrowded city, work long days, for very little money. to work in a clothing factory. to understand why so many people making a choice just like Shumi and Minu. you have to go back to the village where they came from.
